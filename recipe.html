Delicious Pancakes

this simple Recipe will show you how to make fluffy and tasty pancake in just a few steps.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 egg

Instructions

  1. Mix the flour and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Add the milk and egg, then stir until smooth.
  3. Heat each side until golden brown, then serve hot.
  4. Heat a pan and pour a small amount of butter.
